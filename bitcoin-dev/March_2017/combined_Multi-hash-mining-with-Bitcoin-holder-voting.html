Summary:

Adam Perlow and Nathan Cook have proposed a new method that combines multi-hash with bitcoin stake voting to mine blocks under each hash function. The proposal suggests targeting a ratio of blocks mined under each hash function and analyzing the voting on this ratio in terms of "voting on a simplex." This approach is similar to difficulty targeting and utilizes existing research to choose a good method for doing so.This new method offers existing miners a way back in after a contentious hard fork, utilizing their hardware. The full proposal can be found at http://topynate.net/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/proportionateresponse.pdf (archived at http://www.webcitation.org/6pEZLlZoW).
