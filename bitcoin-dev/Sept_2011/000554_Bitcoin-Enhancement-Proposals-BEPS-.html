The author has adapted Python PEP 0001 to Bitcoin and made its license public domain. The Bitcoin Enhancement Proposals (BEP) is open to additional authors and revisions, and the author suggests having a standardisation track for changes to the protocol. The BEP 0001 can be found on the Bitcoin wiki page and ideally, new proposals should be added to the Github repository at github.com/bitcoin/beps/.
