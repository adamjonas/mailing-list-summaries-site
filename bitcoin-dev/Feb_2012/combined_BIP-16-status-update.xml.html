Summary:

The mining support for BIP 16 is still under 50%, and it has been proposed to have a new evaluation/switchover date as it won't be over 50% by March 1. The re-evaluation of the support will take place on March 15th, and the target switchover date will be April 1st. Those already supporting BIP16 are advised to restart bitcoind with the argument '-paytoscripthashtime=1333238400' to delay switchover until April 1st. Tycho has informed Gavin Andresen that deepbit would support BIP16 soon after merging and testing changes, which will increase the support to well over 55%. It is hoped that this will be the last delay in switchover.
