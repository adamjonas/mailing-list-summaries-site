Summary:

Gavin Andresen, a prominent figure in the Bitcoin community, has announced that he will be hosting a chat on #bitcoin-dev on Freenode IRC tomorrow at 21:00 UTC to discuss several important topics related to the cryptocurrency. Among these topics are the status of BIP 16 support, progress towards achieving 50% hashing power, and a protocol change coming up on February 20th regarding checksums in version messages. Additionally, Andresen plans to address the issue of duplicate coinbase and propose a solution involving the inclusion of block height in the coinbase. After discussing these technical matters, attendees are invited to exchange bitcoins with addresses featuring cute Valentine's Day messages.
