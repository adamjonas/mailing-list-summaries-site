Summary:

Bitcoin Improvement Proposal 21, commonly known as BIP 21, has been widely accepted among major implementations in the crypto world. More information about this can be found on the Bitcoin Wiki page for BIP 21. On the other hand, BIP 19 is a proposal to add a new payment type to the scripting system's template list. The details of this proposal can be found on the Bitcoin Wiki page for BIP 19. While the person speaking about it has not fully evaluated it yet, they believe it seems solid. However, they do have concerns about the use of arithmetic operators which can behave in strange ways.
