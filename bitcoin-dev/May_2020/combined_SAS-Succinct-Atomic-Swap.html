However, users need to remember secrets to access their funds, and there is an online/watchtower requirement for the timelock supporting chain. The protocol involves pre-signing transactions and requires the use of pubkeys and secrets to lock up funds. On the other hand, Statechains allow users to create a "Statechain" where they can send Bitcoin to other users who have access to the same set of private keys.
