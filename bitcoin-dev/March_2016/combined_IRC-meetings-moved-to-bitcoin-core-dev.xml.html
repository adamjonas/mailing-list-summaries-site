Summary:

Recently, the weekly developer meeting for Bitcoin has been held in #bitcoin-core-dev instead of its usual location, #bitcoin-dev. This change is due to MeetingBot being deactivated in #bitcoin-dev. The meetings occur every Thursday from 19:00-20:00 UTC and have been taking place in the new location for the past two weeks. Wladimir, one of the developers, has requested that this information be left as is without any further action. It is important for those involved in the development of Bitcoin to be aware of this shift in location.
