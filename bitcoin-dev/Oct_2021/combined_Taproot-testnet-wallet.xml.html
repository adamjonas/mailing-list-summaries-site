Summary:

Andreas Schildbach is seeking a testnet wallet that can receive to P2TR and spend back. He has been trying to get a taproot address from Bitcoin Core 22.0 without success. Pieter Wuille suggested constructing a taproot-capable wallet in Bitcoin Core by creating a descriptor wallet, importing a taproot descriptor, and getting a new address with addresstype=bech32m. Wuille also provided step-by-step instructions on how to construct the taproot descriptor per BIP 86. This process is deliberately cumbersome prior to taproot activating on mainnet; once that happens and PR#22364 is merged, only steps (1) and (5) will be required.
