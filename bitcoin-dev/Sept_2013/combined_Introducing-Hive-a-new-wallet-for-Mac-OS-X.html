Summary:

The team behind BitcoinKit.framework has announced the release of their first test version of Hive, an OS X wallet that is focused on usability and discovery. However, it still lacks many basic features, so it is not yet ready for serious use. The team is seeking feedback and assistance from the community to further improve the wallet. The new version of BitcoinKit.framework now features bitcoinj support. If anyone wants to include an app for their Bitcoin-supporting service in Hive, they can contact the team through grabhive.com or twitter.com/grabhive.
