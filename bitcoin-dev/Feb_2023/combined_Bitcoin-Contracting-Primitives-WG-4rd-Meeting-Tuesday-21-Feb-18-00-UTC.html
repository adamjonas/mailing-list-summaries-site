Summary:

Antoine Riard has announced the fourth Bitcoin contracting primitives working group meeting, which will be held on Tuesday, February 21st at 18:00. The meeting is open to anyone interested in participating and discussing topics related to contracting protocols. Participants can propose additional agenda topics in advance through the GitHub issue tracker. The communication venue is #bitcoin-contracting-primitives-wg on Libera Chat IRC. Two topics have already been proposed for the upcoming meeting. Firstly, ANYPREVOUT / "Eltoo", which is a work in progress during the last year for Eltoo lightning channels. Secondly, setting up an open meatspace event during S2 2023 or S1 2024 if there is sufficient interest. Meatspace events can be a great thing to accelerate the development pace of contracting protocols. If time remaining, everyone blockers in their contracting primitives/covenant research could be listened to. Logs of previous sessions are also available for reference. It is an excellent opportunity for those who are interested in Bitcoin contracting primitives to participate and learn from experienced professionals.
