In a recent discussion, Gavin Andresen stated that the idea of activating a non-proportional demurrage for Bitcoin is not feasible as it would result in negative publicity. He believes that any form of demurrage will never happen and urged people to give up on the idea. According to him, the negative publicity surrounding "the Bitcoin developers are destroying YOUR coins!" would be devastating.
