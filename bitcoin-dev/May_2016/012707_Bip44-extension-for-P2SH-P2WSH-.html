Summary:

In an email to the bitcoin-dev mailing list, Andreas Schildbach discusses the idea behind BIP43 and BIP44. BIP43 defines how balance retrieval works, and BIP44 is based on this idea. The purpose of these BIPs is to ensure that the same balance is always seen on wallets that use the same BIP and seed. Pavol Rusnak expresses gratitude for Schildbach's explanation.
