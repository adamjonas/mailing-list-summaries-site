Summary:

Bitcoin developer Jeremy Rubin has announced the scheduling of a recurring meeting every two weeks on Tuesdays at 12:00 PM PT starting January 11th. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss BIP-119 review and deployment topics, and participants are encouraged to send in topics they would like to cover beforehand for inclusion in the preliminary agenda. Meetings will be moderated tightly to remain on topic while covering all relevant issues. Logs will be available via gnusha. Participation in these meetings does not signify endorsement of BIP-119's inclusion or any specific roadmap but rather aims to carry on the 'gradient descent process' of consensus in an open, interactive, and productive format. Interested parties can drop the author a private note or reply if they plan to attend.
