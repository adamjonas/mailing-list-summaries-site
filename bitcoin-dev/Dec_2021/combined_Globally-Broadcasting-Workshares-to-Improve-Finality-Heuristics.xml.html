Summary:

During a conversation with Nic Carter, Jeremy Rubin proposed a concept related to Bitcoin Core that could provide more accurate finality estimates and warnings if hashrate abandons the chain tip. By setting up Bitcoin Core to gossip headers for work shares, the system would be able to notice missing work, which is observable regardless of whether dishonest miners choose to publish their work on non-tip shares. The GUI could then provide users with additional warning if they accept payment during a sudden hashrate decrease, advising them to wait longer before accepting the payment.
