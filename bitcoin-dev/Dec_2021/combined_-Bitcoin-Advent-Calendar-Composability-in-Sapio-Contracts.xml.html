Jeremy Rubin's latest post discusses how Sapio modules can be used in a composably manner and invites feedback on this active area of research for the Sapio platform. However, he expresses dissatisfaction with the "JSONSchema Type System" despite its remarkable flexibility. According to him, a better type system could enforce guarantees more strongly and introduce formal correctness. Comparing it to things like ERC-20, Eth interfaces aren't particularly binding as functions could do anything.
