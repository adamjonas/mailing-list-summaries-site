Summary:

In his latest blog post, Jeremy Rubin provides code demos of Dutch auctions of NFTs with royalties and discusses other exciting concepts related to non-fungible tokens. He highlights a particularly novel combination of attestation chains, lightning invoices, and NFTs that can create off-chain updatable and on-chain sellable in-game items. The blog post is available at https://rubin.io/bitcoin/2021/12/19/advent-22/.
