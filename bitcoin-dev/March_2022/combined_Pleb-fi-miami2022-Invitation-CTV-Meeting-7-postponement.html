Developers who are interested in building Bitcoin projects using Rust programming language and with a focus on Sapio have been invited to participate in pleb.fi/miami2022. The two-day hackathon event will be held on April 4th and 5th near Miami. However, due to the CTV Meeting scheduling overlap, the host Jeremy Rubin has expressed his regret for not being able to host it. As a result, meeting #7 will resume at the time when meeting #8 would have taken place.
