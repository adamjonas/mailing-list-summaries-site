Summary:

In a discussion on the bitcoin-dev mailing list, Gregory Maxwell and Rusty Russell debated on the timeline for the activation of SegWit. Maxwell suggested that a six-month timeout after release would be more reasonable than the current BIP149 timeout. Russell agreed and proposed setting the activation date on December 16th or January 16th to avoid conflict with year-end holidays. Russell also noted that the delay caused by the debate over the activation mechanism has pushed back the SegWit activation by one to two months. The discussion revolved around the density required to make a stable transition and the speed of adoption of SegWit. It was pointed out that while SegWit has seen widespread adoption, it is important to consider the specifics of the proposal before determining the activation date.
