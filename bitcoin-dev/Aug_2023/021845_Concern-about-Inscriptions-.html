Upon further investigation, it has been realized that the issue with blockchain synchronization lies in the "initial blockchain sync" time rather than storage. Storage does play a significant role in this process.UTreeXO has emerged as a promising solution for syncing the chain more efficiently without the need to download and verify everything. The concern about pricing space in the UTXO set has been raised due to its potential impact on UTXO management, privacy, and doxing.The proposal suggests that pricing UTXOs based on resource costs might eliminate the usage of inscriptions, but implementing such a mechanism could be offensive to many Bitcoin users.In another discussion, Daniel Lipshitz clarifies some aspects related to GAP600's research and services.
