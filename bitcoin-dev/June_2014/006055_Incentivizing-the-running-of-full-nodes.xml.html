Summary:

The email thread discusses incentivizing full nodes in the Bitcoin network. Mike Hearn proposes a reward system based on transaction fees or coin age, and Matt Whitlock raises concerns about perverse incentives and the likelihood of a network split between capitalist and socialist nodes. Justus Ranvier suggests that there are already multiple independent transport networks for Bitcoin, including ipv4, ipv6, Tor, and native_i2p, and as long as multihomed hosts act as bridges, information will propagate across all of them.
