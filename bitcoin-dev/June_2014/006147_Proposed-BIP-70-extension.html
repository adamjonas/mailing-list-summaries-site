Summary:

The conversation revolves around the need for making the reference price for discounts clear to merchants who provide different payment methods. The concern is that payment processors may not be aware of the other payment options available to the merchant and their specific conditions. Currently, Coinbase allows merchants to specify the size of their discount, and they communicate this information to the payment processor. Despite this, the need for transparency regarding reference prices remains a valid point.
