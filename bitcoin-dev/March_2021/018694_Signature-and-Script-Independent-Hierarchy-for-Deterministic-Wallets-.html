Robert Spigler, a developer, proposed a BIP (Bitcoin Improvement Proposal) for multisignature and single signature wallets. However, ghost43 and Jochen Hoenicke raised concerns regarding the proposal's impact on single signature wallets. The updated proposal can be found on Github, along with Spigler's personal fingerprint.
