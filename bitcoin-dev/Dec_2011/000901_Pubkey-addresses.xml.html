Summary:

QR codes have a length limitation and hence a field called "green_address_list" is allowed to specify acceptable addresses in Firstbit format directly in the QR code. The "green_address_details" mechanism is used only when it starts getting too long to fit comfortably into the QR code. In 2011/12/17, Luke-Jr suggested standardizing and supporting public key addresses as they are a better fit for large QR Codes.
