Summary:

Rick Wesson expressed his support for using secured zones to publish TXT records to digital currencies on December 14, 2011. He suggested using the TXT format for Bitcoin with the prefix _btc. However, he warned against confusing BTC (Bitcoin unit) with BC (Bitcoin in general/protocol). The challenge of using DNS for this purpose would be following the best practice of utilizing a new address for each transaction.
