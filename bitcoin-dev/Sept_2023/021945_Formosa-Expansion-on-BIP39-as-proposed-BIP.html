Summary:

Yuri S Villas Boas sent an email to Mr. Dash Jr, Kalle Alm, and other members of the Bitcoin dev mailing list. In the email, Yuri mentioned that after receiving constructive feedback and working on the project for a few more months, he is now ready to formally file "Formosa" as a Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP). Yuri provided documentation for Formosa according to BIP2, which can be found at this link: https://github.com/Yuri-SVB/formosa/blob/master/bip.mediawiki. He also included additional references that can be accessed through this link: https://linktr.ee/formosat3.Yuri expressed his gratitude in advance for the time, attention, and dedication of everyone involved. The farewell part of the email was not included in the summary.
