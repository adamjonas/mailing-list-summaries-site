Summary:

The discussion on Bitcoin-dev mailing list is about the interpretation of BIP65. The original post questions whether funds can be frozen using BIP65. The response explains that the user voluntarily freezes their own funds and that wallets only display funds paid to scriptpubkeys they have generated or would have generated. This means there is no risk of someone sneaking in CLTV locked funds, except through software bugs.
