Summary:

The Bitcoin Core team has announced Jonas Schnelli as the new GUI maintainer. Schnelli has been actively involved in redesigning icons, visualizing network statistics, and improving user experience for the past year. He has also been unofficially giving direction on GUI matters, making his appointment official. As the new GUI maintainer, he will handle GUI-related issues on the GitHub tracker, and assist in merging GUI-related pull requests. This development is seen as a significant step towards addressing the much-needed improvement in the user experience of Bitcoin Core.
