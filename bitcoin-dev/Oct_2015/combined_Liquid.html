In October 2015, Benjamin expressed surprise and confusion over an announcement made by Blockstream to implement Sidechains for exchanges. He questioned the use of terms such as "settlement" and "capital requirements" in relation to Bitcoin, which he deemed as anonymous digital cash with no concept of settlement. Additionally, he inquired about the connection between Bitcoin and existing financial institutions, market liquidity, and commercial products of Blockstream. The email thread discusses the implementation of sidechains for exchanges and raises questions about their relevance to Bitcoin, launch partners such as Bitfinex, BTCC, Kraken, Unocoin, and Xapo, and potential benefits for the industry. Daniel clarifies that Benjamin's questions don't make sense because he's conflating Bitcoin with Blockstream/Liquid. He confirms that Blockstream does have commercial products and that implementing sidechains for exchanges was always part of their plan. Adam suggests that Benjamin read a Reddit thread on Liquid and post his questions there if they're not answered.
