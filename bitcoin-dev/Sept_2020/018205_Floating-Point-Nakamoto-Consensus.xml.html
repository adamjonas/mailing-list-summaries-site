Summary:

In a Bitcoin-dev thread, Mike Brooks discussed the benefits of using fitness tests to prevent block replacement. He noted that with a fitness test, there is a 100% chance of a new block being accepted, and only a 50% or less chance for replacing a block which has already been mined. This incentivizes moving forward. The topic of fitness tests has been previously discussed in response to the selfish mining paper. A proposal from 2013 included responses highlighting the challenges of replacing first seen as the tip disambiguation method. There may be more related discussion on BitcoinTalk.
