Summary:

The Breaking Bitcoin 2019 conference was held recently and Bryan took some notes on the selected topics. Carl Dong gave an excellent talk on guix as a replacement for the gitian build system, the link to which is provided in the context. The mempool analysis, client-side filtering, client updates, privacy and coinjoin talks were also discussed at the conference and transcripts of these talks can be found in the links provided. Hardware wallets were also discussed with talks on extracting seeds from hardware wallets and the future of hardware wallets. Bitcoin upgrades and p2pool analysis were also part of the conference. The lightning network was discussed with talks on routing security, topological analysis, and security panel. Lastly, there was a talk on defense of bitcoin which might be of general interest.
