Summary:

In an email conversation between Peter Todd and Russell O'Connor, they discuss the computation of package-fee rates of mempool transactions. O'Connor suggests that CPFP may already be computing these rates. Todd agrees but points out that this calculation is only done in the miner code and not in the mempool, which could make it difficult to implement. The conversation then shifts to whether or not the package fee rate is considered when ejecting transactions from the bottom of the mempool due to size constraints.
