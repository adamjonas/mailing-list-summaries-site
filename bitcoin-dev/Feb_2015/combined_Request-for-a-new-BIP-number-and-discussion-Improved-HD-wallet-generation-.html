He welcomes any criticism and help with making the BIP more secure. The proposal suggests managing normal private keys hierarchically instead of generating millions of keys ahead of time and storing them on USBs or similar devices. Jona believes that services like "Reality Keys" could be a perfect use case for this modification. He requests any cryptographers to review the math and see if it's sound.
