Doug Huff is requesting permission from Laszlo to include his Info.plist and BitcoinAppIcon.icns into the upstream source repository. The inclusion would enable automation of the osx build without external requirements aside from lib deps, and Doug inquires about the current license for the mentioned files. He asks if they are licensed under MIT or a similar license or if Laszlo would be willing to put them under the Public Domain or another compatible license. Two digital signature messages, smime.p7s and PGP.sig, are attached to the email.
