Mark, a developer, spent some time with the bitcoin-qt client and autotools on Mac. He made small changes in the qt client which builds and runs fine. He requests anyone with these development environments and willingness to test it, to come forward. In conclusion, discussions were held between developers regarding bitcoin-qt client and autotools on different platforms. They aim to merge autotools and qtgui but need more testing before doing so.
