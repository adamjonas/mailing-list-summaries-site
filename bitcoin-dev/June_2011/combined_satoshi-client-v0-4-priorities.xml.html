Summary:

Jeff Garzik, from exMULTI, Inc. mentioned that 'wallet crypto' and 'wallet import/export' are the two "must have" features for v0.4 of Bitcoin. Wallet crypto is an obvious feature for wallet security but the import/export feature might need a rebase. It is important for further wallet security, such as printing out and storing a private key in a bank's safety deposit box. Jeff Garzik believes that autotools and other stuff can wait if they hold up the release of wallet crypto. In the conversation, sipa was already working on rebasing his showwallet tree. However, it was missing the segfault fix; but said fix merges cleanly. Doug Huff also confirmed that it is in a functional state.The conversation also included an advertisement for Splunk, which takes data generated in IT infrastructure and makes sense of it. The email signature contained a digital signature of the sender.
