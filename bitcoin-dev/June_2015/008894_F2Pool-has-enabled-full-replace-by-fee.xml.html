In an email conversation, an individual asked why intentionally making and publicly announcing incompatible promises regarding the disposition of particular Bitcoins under one's control shouldn't be assumed to be a fraud attempt unless shown otherwise. Bitcoin core developer and open source evangelist Jeff Garzik responded by stating that making multiple incompatible versions of a spend is actually a requirement of various refund contract protocols. Garzik works for BitPay, Inc., a bitcoin payment processor.
