Summary:

There is currently a high transaction rate in the blockchain network, resulting in nearly all blocks being full. Blockchain.info reports that this issue is causing limitations, and to address it, there have been suggestions to increase the default max block size to 1MB. This will provide more space for the 731MB blocks and prevent individuals who do not change their settings from the default option from causing any limitations. The purpose of this suggestion is to avoid being limited by people who are not paying attention to the ongoing discussions regarding this matter.
