One of the main problems is the cost of validation, which makes it difficult to construct short proofs. Currently, there is no mechanism for compensating validators, and even full validators that go offline for a short period of time take a while to catch up to the network. The community has discussed hard-forking to remove the 1MB limit on block size, but this could result in a severe market price correction.
