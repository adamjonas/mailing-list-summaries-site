Summary:

Andrew Miller has announced the Cryptocurrency Implementers' Workshop, which will be co-hosted with Financial Crypto. The workshop is aimed at focusing on real-world experience and engineering efforts in the field of cryptocurrency. The program will consist of two main components: Invited talks on implementation post mortems and Artifact Evaluation, and Demo Show and Tell. The technical program will feature demo presentations about open-source software artifacts. The suggested topics for the workshop are cryptography implementations, proof-of-work consensus protocols, proof-of-stake consensus protocols, mining hardware designs, peer-to-peer networking and broadcast relay code, full node synchronization, SPV and lightweight clients, wallets and key management, security vulnerabilities and defenses, mining pools, mining rig controllers, and staking wallets. The submission deadline is Dec 21, and the presenter notification will be given on Jan 7. The workshop will be held on Feb 22. The program committee includes J.
