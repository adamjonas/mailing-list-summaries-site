Summary:

The email exchange between Alan Reiner and Richard Brady, dated January 19, 2015, discusses the use of protobuf in BIP70. Reiner expresses confusion around the determinism properties of protobuf, stating that he thought there was no guarantee that two identical protobuf structures would get serialized identically. In response, it is explained that while protobuf does not have deterministic serialization, BIP70 passes around serialized protobuf data that is signed directly because it can easily be a byte array with json instead.
