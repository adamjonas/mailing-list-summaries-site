Summary:

In an email from 2015, Gavin Andresen discusses updating the "DER standard" and provides a link to the 2002 revision. He suggests linking to http://www.itu.int/rec/T-REC-X.690-200811-I/en, which has the latest revision (Nov. 2008) and is more visible to people searching for X.690. Andresen also recommends changes to some of the language used in the proposal, such as substituting "very few transactions" for "nearly no transactions" and clarifying the meaning of "opcode failure". Douglas Roark, Senior Developer at Armory Technologies, Inc., responds and agrees with Andresen's suggestions.
