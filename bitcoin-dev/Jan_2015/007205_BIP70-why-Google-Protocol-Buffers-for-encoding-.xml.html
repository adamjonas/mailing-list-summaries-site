Summary:

In modifying BIP70 to function with an altcoin, there was consideration of replacing protocol buffers. Changes were required to eliminate any risk that payment requests could be accidentally applied to the wrong blockchain. The idea of serializing a blockchain identifier with messages was proposed, but arbitrarily deviating from a given design choice just for the sake of diversity would not necessarily serve the quality of the blockchain network.
