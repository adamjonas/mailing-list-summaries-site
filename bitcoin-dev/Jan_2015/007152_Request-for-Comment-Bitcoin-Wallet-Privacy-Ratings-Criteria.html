Summary:

The Open Bitcoin Privacy Project has requested public feedback on its rating criteria for Bitcoin wallet privacy. The deadline to provide feedback is Jan 23, 2015. The criteria will be used to evaluate and score various Bitcoin wallets, which will be published on the project's website. Feedback can be provided through the mailing list or via GitHub. The current version of the criteria was authored by members of the OBPP including Kristov Atlas, Justus Ranvier, Chris Pacia, and Samuel Patterson.
