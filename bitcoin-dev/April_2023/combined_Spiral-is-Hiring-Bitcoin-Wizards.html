Spiral, a non-profit Bitcoin organization within a major tech company, is hiring Bitcoin experts for various projects. The organization contributes to Bitcoin by writing code, funding projects and individuals, educating, entertaining, and testing the limits of what a tiny team can do to impact the future of money. Spiral's full-time engineering team has focused on developing LDK, a kit that simplifies how developers add the Lightning Network to apps and wallets. This year, they plan to build an R&D team within Spiral to improve Bitcoin's security, decentralization, privacy, scaling, and expressivity beyond LDK.The job responsibilities include identifying and driving forward multiple projects in collaboration with the Spiral team and the greater Bitcoin development community. Applicants should have a deep technical understanding of Bitcoin and be comfortable engaging on Bitcoin topics and channels such as bitcoin-dev or lightning-dev email lists, local bitdevs, or in a "wizards" channel such as IRC #bitcoin-wizards.
