In a discussion on the bitcoin-dev mailing list, Matt Corallo expressed his belief that "some vocal users" threatening to fork themselves off is not a good justification for technical decisions. He emphasized the need for communication and agreement that a failed BIP 8/9 activation does not mean the end of the story for Taproot activation. If there is broad consensus for Taproot but some miners fail to upgrade in time, he believes a flag day activation would be merited.
