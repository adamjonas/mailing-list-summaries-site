Summary:

The author of the message stated that they have stopped running a full node on their desktop machines due to unbearable sluggishness caused by a bottleneck in IOPS rather than bulk disk space. They also mentioned that running dedicated nodes is fine, but latency is a major issue on desktops compared to servers. The author did not notice any network latency, but their internet is subpar. The author noted that their desktops, although not the newest, still function well without bitcoind running.
