In an email conversation on April 23, 2014, Pavol Rusnak and Luke-Jr discussed the compatibility of single-subwallet clients with Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP) 64. Luke-Jr argued that as long as the user has a way to specify which subwallet they are accessing, there should be no problem with single-subwallet clients. However, Rusnak countered that calling these clients BIP64 compatible would be inaccurate. The conversation then shifted to the restrictiveness of BIP64, with Rusnak stating that most end users do not need subwallet support.
