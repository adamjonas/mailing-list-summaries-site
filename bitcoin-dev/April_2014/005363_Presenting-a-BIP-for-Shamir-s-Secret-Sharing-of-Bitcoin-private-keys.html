The conversation starts with a suggestion to add some prefixes for testnet. The next speaker welcomes forks of their draft BIP but admits they don't have the inclination to research GF(2^8) secret sharing schemes and write an implementation at present. However, they are willing to fork it. The third speaker provides information about the maximum number of shares possible over GF(2^8), which is 255. They suggest this makes M and x biases unnecessary.
