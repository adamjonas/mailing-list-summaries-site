In a conversation on 23rd April 2014, Luke-Jr expressed confusion over the meaning of "account" in Bitcoin after BIP32 redefined it to mean "subwallet." Previously, Bitcoin-qt used independent addresses. Single-subwallet wallet software may now need users to provide a subwallet number in addition to the seed. This could lead to user confusion as they may not be able to see all their funds.
