Summary:

Pieter Wuille, a Bitcoin core developer, proposed a small draft BIP with a proposal to turn Bitcoin into a limited-supply currency. Wuille believes that the current subsidy schedule is no longer acceptable and that changes need to be made to maintain non-inflationary practices. He notes that Dogecoin has already shown how easy such changes are to implement and considers it a worthwhile idea to explore. However, this proposal was met with controversy and objections from other developers who disagreed with Wuille's view. Despite this, he requested a BIP number for his proposal, which was assigned as BIP 42 by Gregory Maxwell. The proposal can be found at https://gist.github.com/sipa/9920696.
