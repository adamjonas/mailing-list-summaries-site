Summary:

The Open Bitcoin Privacy Project has published its first report on Bitcoin wallet privacy features. The report, which is available to read in full, assesses the privacy of a number of popular wallets. The project will produce further reports over time as it establishes itself as a key source of information in the Bitcoin space. The organization is keen to receive feedback from the community regarding its methodology and criteria for assessing privacy.
