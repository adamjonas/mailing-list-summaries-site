Summary:

Dave Collins and Josh Rickmar have found a consensus change in Bitcoin Core that results in forking behavior on regtest. The problem is related to the broken difficulty retargetting of regtest, but this issue doesn't affect testnet or mainnet. Dave Collins posted the details of their discovery on the bitcointalk forums, which can be accessed through the URL https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=1065504.
