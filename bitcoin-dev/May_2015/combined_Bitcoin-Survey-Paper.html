Florian Tschorsch, a research scholar interested in Bitcoin, noticed that there was no comprehensive survey paper on the subject. As a result, he decided to create a technical report on the topic which can serve as a survey paper for Bitcoin enthusiasts. Florian shared the link to the report and invited feedback from readers to improve future revisions. One of the readers, Daniel, suggested that Florian should take a look at another research paper about Bitcoin co-authored by Joseph Bonneau. Daniel also advertised his own work, which focuses on the difficulty mechanism of Bitcoin and is available as an official publication as well as a preprint without paywall. Finally, Daniel wished Florian good luck with further research.
