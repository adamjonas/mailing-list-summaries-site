Summary:

BitPay has released a whitepaper on ChainDB, which is its new peer-to-peer database system supported by the Bitcoin blockchain. The paper outlines BitPay's intended consensus mechanism, proof of fee, which some critics have suggested creates risks. Peter Todd suggests that direct provably unspendable OP_RETURN sacrifices be implemented instead and that ChainDB chains should be private. It is unclear what exactly ChainDB's proposal is attempting to accomplish and prevent from happening. There are also comments on Zookeyv and Factom. Eric Martindale, BitPay's CTO invites any questions about the paper itself and requests feedback from users.
