Summary:

The message shared in the context discusses the BIP proposal related to getmemorypool JSON-RPC method. The recipient seeks an opinion on whether this proposal is related to BIPs or not. The sender explains that BIPs are implementation-specific non-bitcoin-protocol proposals that focus on generic use-cases and the protocol, affecting all clients, allowing the system to function as a united whole. The sender further adds that the proposed change in Bitcoin-Qt's abstraction layer or UI placement would not be appropriate material for BIPs. However, the sender approves of the BIP process for standardizing interoperability of the network and community regarding URIs.
