The BIP16 switchover time of April 1 has been announced as final, with approximately 70% of hashing power supporting it for the last two days and 54% over the last week. Pools and miners who haven't upgraded their software are encouraged to do so in the next two weeks to avoid hashing on a minority chain. Gavin Andresen is drafting a document that suggests how to make the next upgrade process less painful.
