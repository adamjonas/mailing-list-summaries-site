Summary:

The Call for Proposals (CFP) for 'Scaling Bitcoin 2017: Stanford' has been announced and is now open. The event will take place on November 4-5, 2017 in California. Interested applicants can find details about the CFP on the official website https://scalingbitcoin.org. The deadline for submissions to the CFP is September 25th and applicant acceptance notification will be sent out on October 16th.Those who are interested in participating in the event and plan on submitting a proposal should visit https://scalingbitcoin.org/event/stanford2017#cfp for the full CFP. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals and companies to showcase their work and ideas related to scaling Bitcoin. It is a chance for them to demonstrate how they are contributing to the growth and development of Bitcoin.This is a significant event for those involved in the Bitcoin ecosystem. Scaling Bitcoin is an important issue that needs to be addressed in order for the cryptocurrency to continue on its path of growth and adoption. The event brings together developers, researchers, and other stakeholders to discuss and share their ideas on how to scale Bitcoin in a secure and sustainable way.Overall, the CFP for 'Scaling Bitcoin 2017: Stanford' presents an exciting opportunity for those involved in the Bitcoin community to share their knowledge and contribute to the future of this innovative technology. With the deadline for submissions approaching, interested parties should act quickly to ensure they have the opportunity to participate in this important event.
