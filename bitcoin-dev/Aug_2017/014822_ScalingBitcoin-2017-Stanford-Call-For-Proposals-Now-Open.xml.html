Summary:

The Call for Proposals (CFP) for 'Scaling Bitcoin 2017: Stanford' has been announced and is now open. The event will take place on November 4-5, 2017 in California. Interested applicants can find details about the CFP on the official website https://scalingbitcoin.org. The deadline for submissions to the CFP is September 25th and applicant acceptance notification will be sent out on October 16th. Those who are interested in participating in the event and plan on submitting a proposal should visit https://scalingbitcoin.org/event/stanford2017#cfp for the full CFP. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals and companies to showcase their work and ideas related to scaling Bitcoin.
