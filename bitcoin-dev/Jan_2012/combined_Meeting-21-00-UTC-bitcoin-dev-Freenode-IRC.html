Summary:

Amir Taaki has sent an email announcing a meeting to discuss the new OP_EVAL changes coming to Bitcoin, scheduled for tomorrow. The goal of the meeting is to prevent fragmentation and ensure harmony among projects by having regular meetings to propose and debate concepts and algorithms. A wiki page will be created in advance of each weekly meeting with talking points added throughout the week. After the meeting, a log of the discussion will be posted online, and an accessible summary will be typed for the community at large on bitcoinmedia.com. The meeting will take place on Freenode IRC #bitcoin-dev from 21:00 to 22:00 (16:00 New York time). It is expected that the meeting will not go over this hour to avoid off-topic discussions. Additionally, the email contains information on Citrix VDI-in-a-Box, a solution for delivering seamless, secure access to virtual desktops. Taaki hopes that having a scheduled regular platform can achieve more accessibility to Bitcoin development and the system.
