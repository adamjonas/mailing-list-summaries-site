Summary:

In an email exchange dated January 16, 2012, Jeff Garzik, a Bitcoin core developer, expressed his desire for bitcoin.org to remain apolitical and avoid controversial issues that might spark moral outrage. He was responding to concerns about the impact of SOPA (Stop Online Piracy Act) and PIPA (Protect Intellectual Property Act) policy on Bitcoin. Garzik asked those who believed the policies affected Bitcoin to provide evidence of their claims.
