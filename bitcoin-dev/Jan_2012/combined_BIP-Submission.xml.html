Summary:

Ben Reeves has submitted a draft Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP) as an alternative to BIP 16, also known as "/P2SH/". The proposed BIP, named "BIP M of 2 DRAFT", can be found on the Bitcoin Wiki page. The proposal comes in light of a discussion about the limitations of the OP_ADD function in Bitcoin, which is limited to 4-byte operands and cannot be changed without a hard blockchain split or entire-network-upgrade. Gavin Andresen sent a message requesting feedback and comments from the community regarding Reeves' proposed BIP.Reeves seeks discussion and possible formal review of his draft BIP from the Bitcoin community.
