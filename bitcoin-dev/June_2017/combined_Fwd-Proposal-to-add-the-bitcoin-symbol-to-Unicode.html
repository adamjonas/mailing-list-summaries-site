Summary:

Ken Shirriff proposed adding the B-with-vertical-bars bitcoin symbol to Unicode in 2015 and requested endorsement from Bitcoin developers to convince the Unicode Committee. Danny Thorpe suggested using the B-with-horizontal-bar symbol instead, but Ken argued it could cause confusion with the Thai Baht currency symbol. In June 2017, the bitcoin symbol was successfully added to Unicode version 10.0 by a working group, which Theo seems to have been involved in. However, Theo expressed frustration over false claims made by reporters and urged the team to keep them in check.
