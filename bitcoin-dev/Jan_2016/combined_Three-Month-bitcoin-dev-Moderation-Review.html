The Bitcoin-dev mailing list recently had discussions on the usefulness of comments in technical conversations. Members suggested that technical comments should contribute uniquely to the conversation and not simply repeat what has already been said. A simple +1 without any added explanation was deemed unhelpful and not useful. The goal is to provide readers with another thing to consider in favor of something beyond just the number of people who support it. Evidence trumps votes.Rusty Russell questioned the moderation rules that discouraged "+1s" but clarified that providing a technical explanation of agreement is acceptable. Rusty recommended amending the rules to clarify that "+1s" are not allowed without an explanation.
