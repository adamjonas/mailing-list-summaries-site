Summary:

Several companies in the Bitcoin industry have pledged their support for segregated witness (segwit), a major improvement to the Bitcoin protocol. BlockCypher, Breadwallet, Blocktrail, GreenAddress, GreenBits, mSIGNA, and NBitcoin have all committed to supporting segwit. A segregated witness testnet called segnet has been running successfully since December 31, 2015, with several wallets including mSIGNA, GreenAddress, GreenBits, Blocktrail, and NBitcoin committing to supporting it. Breadwallet has also announced its commitment to supporting segwit. BitGo, a digital asset security platform, has already implemented SegWit transactions for Bitcoin and is waiting for its activation on the Bitcoin network before rolling out support to customers. Segwit is expected to provide increased capacity and improved transaction malleability to the Bitcoin network. Wallet developers interested in developing and testing on segnet are encouraged to contact Eric Lombrozo.
