Summary:

The wallet provides encryption support, ensuring that if someone steals the wallet.dat file, they won't be able to access it without a passphrase. This can be done through RPC calls such as 'encryptwallet', 'walletpassphrase', and 'walletlock'. However, adding a noticeable flag to indicate locking or unlocking the wallet may not align with good privacy ethics.
