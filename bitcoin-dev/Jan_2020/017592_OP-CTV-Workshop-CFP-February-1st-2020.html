Summary:

Bitcoin developer Jeremy Rubin is hosting a workshop on February 1st, 2020 in San Francisco to aid in reviewing and advancing OP_CHECKTEMPLATEVERIFY. The workshop will be from 10am-5pm, and the primary audience for the event is Bitcoin developers, ecosystem engineers (i.e., mining pools, wallets, exchanges, etc), and researchers. If interested in attending, participants must fill out an application form by January 15th, and indicate if they require travel sponsorship. The schedule of events includes a BIP session, implementation session, deployment session, ecosystem support session, demo session & application talks, and a wrap-up. Coffee and registration begins at 10:00 AM, and dinner & drinks will be served from 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM. Rubin may be able to sponsor travel for a few developers who would otherwise be unable to attend. Materials from the event will be made available online for those who cannot attend. Participants are encouraged to forward the posting to people who don't follow this list but they think should attend.
