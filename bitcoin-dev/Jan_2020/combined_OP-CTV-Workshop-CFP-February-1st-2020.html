Summary:

Bitcoin developer Jeremy Rubin is hosting a workshop on February 1st, 2020 in San Francisco to advance OP_CHECKTEMPLATEVERIFY. The workshop will be from 10am-5pm and primarily aimed at Bitcoin developers, ecosystem engineers, and researchers. Interested participants must fill out an application form by January 15th and indicate if they require travel sponsorship. Materials from the event will be made available online for those unable to attend. The schedule includes a BIP session, implementation session, deployment session, ecosystem support session, demo session & application talks, and wrap up. Coffee & registration will be held from 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM, and lunch will be served at 12 PM. There will also be a dinner & drinks session from 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM. Rubin may be able to sponsor travel for a few developers who would otherwise be unable to attend. Those interested in presenting research or projects related to OP_CTV can indicate so in the application form with a brief summary of their topic. Participants are encouraged to forward the posting to individuals who don't follow this list but who they think should attend. The channel ##ctv-bip-review is available for general discussion about OP_CTV.
