Summary:

A workshop to advance OP_CHECKTEMPLATEVERIFY is being hosted by Jeremy Rubin on February 1st, 2020 in San Francisco. The event will be from 10am-5pm and primarily aimed at Bitcoin developers, ecosystem engineers, and researchers. Attendees can fill out the form to register for the workshop. Those interested in presenting research or projects related to OP_CTV can indicate so in the application form with a brief summary of their topic. Travel sponsorship may be available for a few developers who would otherwise be unable to attend, and those who are able to sponsor the event can reach out or indicate on the form. The workshop schedule includes BIP session, implementation session, deployment session, ecosystem support session, demo session & application talks, and wrap up. The basic schedule of events is subject to change. Coffee & registration will be held from 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM, and lunch will be served at 12 PM. There will also be a dinner & drinks session from 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM. If attendees cannot attend, materials from the event will be made available online, and the channel ##ctv-bip-review is available for general discussion about OP_CTV.
