In 2012, a discussion was held regarding a Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP) being split into three separate proposals. The discussion took place on a mailing list, not IRC, though it took some prompting on IRC to get Luke-jr to move in the direction of breaking up the proposal. It was suggested that further discussion could be held if anyone else had objections or missed the original discussion.
