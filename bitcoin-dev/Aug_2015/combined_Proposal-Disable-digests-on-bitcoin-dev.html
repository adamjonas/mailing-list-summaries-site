Summary:

Warren Togami Jr. suggested disabling the ability to subscribe with digests on the bitcoin-dev list, arguing that they were from an earlier era of Internet history and inconvenient for everyone because replies have the problem of breaking threads. Instead, he suggested people set mail filters to separate list mail from other mail. He believed that digests were not a net-positive for the community and should be turned off for the benefit of the majority. The proposal was made on September 1, 2015, via bitcoin-dev and may be considered annoying by some, but it was necessary for the betterment of the Bitcoin community.
