Summary:

Bitcoin developers discussed the BIP 72 spec and its updates, including an optional bitcoin address, and concerns about losing metadata and reducing responsibilities of the sender. They also discussed the processing of BIP 21 amount, message, and label parameters when using the payment protocol, and how wallet applications can function without supporting the P2P protocol. Some participants suggested that the receiver should confirm transactions and get them confirmed rather than the sender, while others disagreed. Lastly, they considered discouraging broadcasting before paymentACK is obtained to ensure the merchant receives it with metadata.
