Summary:

The bitcoin-qt application's XDG desktop integration on some desktop environments requests that it be placed under the "Office" menu category, which is a rather broad category. The suggestion is to refine this further into "Finance", given that XDG's menu specification allows for this. The suggested line in bitcoin-qt.desktop should be as follows: Categories=Office;Finance. The author apologizes for not providing the patch himself due to his weak knowledge of Git.
