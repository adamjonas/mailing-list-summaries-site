Summary:

The Call for Proposals (CFP) for 'Scaling Bitcoin 2016: Retarget' has been announced and is now open. Interested applicants can submit their proposals until September 2nd, after which the applicant acceptance notification deadline is September 23rd. The event is scheduled to be held in Milan on October 8th and 9th. However, the deadline for submissions has been extended to September 9th, with rolling acceptance offered this time around to try and get most responses out before the 23rd. The definition of "scaling" at the conference is broad, including topics such as improving Bitcoin throughput, layer 2 ideas, security and privacy, incentives and fee structures, testing, simulation and modeling, network resilience and latency, fungibility, anti-spam measures, block size proposals, and mining concerns. The Call for Proposals (CFP) opened on August 2nd and can be found on the scalingbitcoin.org website. For more details, interested parties may visit https://scalingbitcoin.org.
