Summary:

On August 14th, 2016, a user named Cannon asked the bitcoin-dev mailing list if there was a mailing list specifically for general users of Bitcoin. The bitcoin-dev mailing list is focused on development-related topics, and Cannon was looking for a forum for non-development questions related to Bitcoin. A link was provided in response to Cannon's inquiry, leading to the bitcoin-discuss mailing list intended for general discussion about Bitcoin and related technologies. This list is open to anyone who wants to participate in broader discussions about Bitcoin and its uses. Cannon included their contact information such as email, PGP fingerprint, Bitmessage address, and Ricochet-IM in their communication seeking a more general mailing list for Bitcoin related topics. In response to this inquiry, other users suggested checking out the /r/btc and /r/Bitcoin subreddits on Reddit for more general discussions about Bitcoin. Overall, while the bitcoin-dev mailing list is tailored towards development-specific topics, the bitcoin-discuss mailing list provides a platform for general discussions about Bitcoin and its usage.
