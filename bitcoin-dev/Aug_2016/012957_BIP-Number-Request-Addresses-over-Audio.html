Summary:

Daniel Hoffman proposed a BIP idea for representing Bitcoin addresses over audio. The idea is to take the binary representation of the Bitcoin address, chop it up into 4 or 2 bit chunks and generate a tone based on that value. This can be used for donating to podcasts or other audio content using a Shazam-esque app or media player with this capability. Justin W. Newton, the Founder/CEO of Netki, Inc, responded to Daniel's proposal and suggested adding additional tones to denote an amount optionally in satoshis. The full specification of the BIP is available on Daniel's GitHub page.
