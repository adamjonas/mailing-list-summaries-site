The bitcoin-dev mailing list is primarily focused on development-related topics. This list is open to anyone who wants to participate and discuss various aspects of Bitcoin that may not be directly related to development.Overall, it appears that while the bitcoin-dev mailing list is tailored towards development-specific topics, the bitcoin-discuss mailing list is available for broader discussions about Bitcoin and its uses.
