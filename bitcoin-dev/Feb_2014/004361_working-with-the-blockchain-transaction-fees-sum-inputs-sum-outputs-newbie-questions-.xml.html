The poster is requesting technical explanations of transaction fees and how to find them and account for them in the blockchain. They also ask what other cases might exist where the sum of the input values can be different from the sum of the output values. The only two cases the poster currently understands are "coinbase" transactions that print money and have no inputs, and transactions fees when in > out.
