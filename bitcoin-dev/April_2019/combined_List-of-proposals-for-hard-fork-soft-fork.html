Summary:

A list of potential softfork proposals for SegWit v1 has been provided, which includes Schnorr signatures, MuSig, Taproot, SIGHASH_NOINPUT, signature aggregation, and MAST. It is not clear whether this list is exhaustive. Among these proposals, only SIGHASH_NOINPUT has a BIP (BIP 118), but it may not match the version that will eventually reach Bitcoin Core. To avoid incorrect use of SIGHASH_NOINPUT, it is suggested that every input signed with a SIGHASH_NOINPUT signature should also require a signature without SIGHASH_NOINPUT. However, further details are not available.Lord His Excellency James HRMH had requested a list of solid proposals for future consensus-driven forks to build momentum for valuable implementations. Thus, it is suggested that a list of strong proposals and BIPs be created for inclusion in a future consensus-driven fork. This would provide momentum for valuable implementations that may otherwise not gain traction. While not every proposal will be considered, pre-consensus voting could help determine what should be included in the fork packages.
