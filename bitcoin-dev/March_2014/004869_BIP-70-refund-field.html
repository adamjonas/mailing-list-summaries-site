In an email exchange between Andreas Schildbach and an unidentified recipient, Schildbach questions the use of PaymentDetails as a solution to a problem. The recipient suggests that communication between the merchant and buyer would be necessary in cases where a refund is needed after a year or more, noting that people's contact information may change over time.
