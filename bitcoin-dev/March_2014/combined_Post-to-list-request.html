Summary:

On March 21, 2014, Chris D'Costa requested access to post on the dev list for his project, the Meek hardware wallet. The purpose of the project was to develop a solution that would prevent Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) attacks while transmitting pay-to information over a non-secure transport mechanism. This request may have been made in order to seek feedback or collaboration from the developer community.In addition to the request, D'Costa provided a link to download a free O'Reilly book called "Graph Databases" in the email signature. The book serves as a guide to graph databases and their applications, and was written by three acclaimed leaders in the field.In response to the request, Andreas Schildbach welcomed D'Costa's access to post on the dev list. The email thread also included a promotional message for the free book download on graph databases. The email was sent to the Bitcoin-development mailing list managed by SourceForge.net.
