In an email conversation from 03/29/2014 01:19 PM, Matt Whitlock expressed his intentional omission of the parameter M (minimum subset size) from shares because including it would give an adversary crucial information. He believed that providing any information that would allow a determination of whether the secret has been reconstructed correctly would give an adversary too much information. The failing silently when given incorrect shares or an insufficient number of shares was also intentional. If not, they know they need more fragments.
