Summary:

Payward Inc. has submitted a draft to the IETF proposing an Internet IBAN (IIBAN) that would identify internet-based financial endpoints in a way that is superset-compatible with the existing European Committee for Banking Standards (ECBS) International Bank Account Number (IBAN) standard. The abstract of the draft can be found on the IETF website at https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-iiban. The proposed IIBAN aims to help streamline online financial transactions. Walter Stanish, representing Payward, Inc., signed off on the submission.
