Summary:

The Bitcoin Contracting Primitives Working Group is set to hold their second meeting on Tuesday, December 20th at 18:00 UTC. The agenda for the meeting includes discussing contracting protocol use-cases and corresponding primitives to ensure completeness of past community ideas. Members are encouraged to open a PR or reach out to the group leader if they have been working on a primitive or use-case that is missing from the current listing. The second part of the meeting will involve listening to everyone's blockers regarding their contracting primitives/covenant research and providing support where needed. The communication venue for the group is #bitcoin-contracting-primitives-wg on Libera Chat, and logs from the previous session are available on GitHub. One participant has plans to work on a taproot annex implementation as a fee-bumping primitive and constrained amount malleability extension for multi-party channels. Antoine Riard, a member of the Bitcoin contracting primitives working group, will lead the meeting and his personal goal is to keep rolling the ball on the aforementioned implementation.
