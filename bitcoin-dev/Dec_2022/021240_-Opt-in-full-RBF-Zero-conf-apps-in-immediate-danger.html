Summary:

Max Krupyshev, the founder and leader of Coinspaid, a Berlin-based cryptocurrency processing company, sent an email to Daniel Lipshitz, a representative from GAP600, discussing the advantages of Bitcoin zero conf for their business. Krupyshev confirmed that Coinspaid runs a large Bitcoin payment operation with over 300k incoming transactions and 700+ inputs a month, which is a significant percentage of the overall BTC chain capacity. He expressed his satisfaction with the zero conf flows provided by GAP600, stating that their clients like to offer instant experiences to their end customers. Krupyshev also mentioned that he did not want to disappoint their clients with news that "waiting for confirmations" may be on the table again after years of a smooth experience. The email included two main production clusters that are rotating around: 3JodN7GmkHdPgKj9G7HCkn9NDLhrcWCjVN and 3QKCocNhzAgtgFLsD5qUZcG6e4TkfRf421. The feedback from Coinspaid's CEO highlights the importance of providing a seamless payment experience in the cryptocurrency industry, where speed and efficiency are crucial factors to consider.
