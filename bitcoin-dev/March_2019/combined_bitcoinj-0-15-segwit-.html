Summary:

The latest update of Bitcoinj, version 0.15, now supports native segregated witness as per a recent post on Google groups. The team has also released new versions 7.0 of Bitcoin Wallet to test the functionality of the new update. Although it creates a keychain of legacy addresses, it can send to native segwit addresses through Bech32 and is available on Testnet and Mainnet, with the latter requiring users to opt into beta to receive 7.x. The team is working on the ability to receive and spend from native segwit addresses which will be released in the next few days and weeks.
