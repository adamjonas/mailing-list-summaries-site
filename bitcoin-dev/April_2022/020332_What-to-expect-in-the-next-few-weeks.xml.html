The mailing list appears to be bypassed in favor of personal blogs and messaging apps, but the author promises to keep the list informed if necessary. The author encourages interested parties to join ##ursf on Libera IRC if they are interested in the effort to resist the contentious soft fork activation attempt of CTV.
