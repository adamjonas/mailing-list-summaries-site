Summary:

The email discusses the potential benefits and drawbacks of using futures markets in Bitcoin development. While they can provide information and serve as a hedge, they may also be misused or manipulated. The writer argues that Bitcoin development should not be solely determined by those funding it, but rather be negotiated between development and the market. Prediction markets are suggested as a way to assist in this negotiation process, though they cannot replace Bitcoin development. The email concludes by emphasizing the importance of considering a wide range of sources when determining the direction of Bitcoin development, including the Bitcoin Dev Mailing List, GitHub repositories, IRC channels, and conversations with stakeholders. Additionally, a blog post by Bitcoin developer Prayank is referenced, which suggests that a chatroom requiring payments in satoshis could be used as a better method for filtering out noise while supporting developers. Ultimately, the email acknowledges the challenges in negotiating the development of Bitcoin amidst competing interests and limited resources.
