Summary:

Bitcoin's UTXO set growth may be limited by adding a block weight penalty for UTXO creation and a bonus for UTXO destruction. The net change in UTXOs within each block could determine the weight limit and reduce potential transaction fees. Three possible solutions have been proposed, including letting any excess be wasted, decreasing the weight limit slightly, or increasing the weight limit if miners consolidate enough UTXOs. However, this can make low-fee transactions harder to confirm, and miners might create dust UTXOs to free up capacity. There are already UTXO consolidation incentives that exist, but privacy concerns exist with post coinjoin. Prayank was unsure about some of Yanmaani's proposal, including the part about fees decreasing due to smaller blocks, as they believe that fees should increase if such blocks are regularly mined and predictable.
