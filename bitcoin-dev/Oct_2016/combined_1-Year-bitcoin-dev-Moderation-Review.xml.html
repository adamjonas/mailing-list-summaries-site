This has led to a request for information on subscription counts for both bitcoin-discuss and bitcoin-dev mailing lists. An opt-in system requires individuals to actively choose to subscribe to a particular group or mailing list, while an opt-out system automatically subscribes individuals unless they take action to unsubscribe.
