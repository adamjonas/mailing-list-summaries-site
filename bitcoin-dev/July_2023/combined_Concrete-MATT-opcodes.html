Johan seeks Salvatore's thoughts on these suggestions.The email also mentions a proposal for the core opcodes of MATT by Salvatore Ingala. The code is implemented in the bitcoin-inquisition repository fork and includes OP_CHECKCONTRACTVERIFY. The changes compared to the previous draft include replacing OP_CHECK{IN, OUT}CONTRACTVERIFY with a single opcode, introducing an additional `flags` parameter, modifying the order of parameters, and defining special values and flags. The author notes that while some special values may not be useful in applications, they were included for flexibility. With the new opcode, the full generality of MATT can be achieved with just two opcodes: OP_CHECKCONTRACTVERIFY and OP_CAT. Additional opcodes and introspection may benefit certain applications, and the author looks forward to comments and starting a BIP proposal.Overall, the email exchange covers discussions on mitigating risks in off-chain contracts, introducing advanced bitcoin contracts, and proposing improvements to the core opcodes of MATT.
