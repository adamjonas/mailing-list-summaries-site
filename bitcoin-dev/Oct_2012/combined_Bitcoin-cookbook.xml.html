Summary:

Jeff Garzik, the CEO of exMULTI, Inc., has started a forum thread on Bitcointalk.org called "Bitcoin cookbook" with the aim of gathering real-world examples of advanced uses of Bitcoin in a step-by-step format. The idea is for contributors to create a new thread detailing their recipe and then mention it in the cookbook. The ultimate goal is for some of these recipes to be migrated to the wiki. However, the wiki is not an ideal place for discussion and questions. This initiative seeks to facilitate the sharing of practical knowledge about advanced uses of Bitcoin.
