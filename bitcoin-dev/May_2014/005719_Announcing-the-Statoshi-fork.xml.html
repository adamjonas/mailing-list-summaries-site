Statsd expects a simple packet format, making it easy to experiment with. To further improve the patch, it is suggested that someone can obtain a feed of the stats packets via TCP by using ssh tunnelling to their host. Once a feed of simple stats packets is easily accessible, individuals can try different kinds of aggregation and monitoring software from their desktops.
