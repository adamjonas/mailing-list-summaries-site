Summary:

The Bitcoin project is seeking volunteers to improve translations in various languages, with the release candidate for 0.9.2 scheduled for May 13th, 2014. Non-native speakers are encouraged to join Transifex to help clean up Bitcoin and Litecoin translations. Volunteers can try nightly builds of Bitcoin Core while heading towards 0.9.2, although it is not recommended for their production wallet. Joining the Translator mailing list is also necessary to receive announcements when translations are needed for Bitcoin, as well as notifications if other Bitcoin Project things in Transifex need translations. Even those who do not speak other languages can help by pointing others who do to the translation page.
