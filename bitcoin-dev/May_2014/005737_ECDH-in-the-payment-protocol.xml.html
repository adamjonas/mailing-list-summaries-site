Summary:

The speaker is discussing the benefits of using atomicity in transactions, which allows for payments that use no extra space in the blockchain and still maintain security in case of communication failure. They suggest that lightweight clients can identify these transactions without markers. Additionally, they mention that extending BIP70 with ECDH would make sense in order to enable other useful features such as messages and refund addresses.
