One person in the conversation points out that assuming knowledge of what statsd is, could be an issue. The discussion then moves onto setting up a monitored bitcoind setup, which seems like a lot of work. The idea is raised to simplify the process by running a community statsd and graphite instance, allowing for aggregate statistics across many nodes without individual node operators having to set everything up themselves.
