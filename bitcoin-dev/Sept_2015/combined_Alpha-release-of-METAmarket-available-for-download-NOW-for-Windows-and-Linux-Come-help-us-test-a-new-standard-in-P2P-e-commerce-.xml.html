Summary:

METAmarket is a new decentralized e-commerce platform that allows users to have complete control over their own marketplace, whether it be open to all or private, wholesale or retail, moderated or unmoderated, clearnet or darknet. The platform uses a zero-fee approach and ensures funds are secure from theft or exit scams with a P2P Bitcoin multisig escrow between buyer and seller only. METAmarket is built directly on top of the Bitcoin and Bitmessage reference clients, prioritizing security above all else. The platform offers a customizable experience and is built on a peer-to-peer model. Users can choose the type of marketplace they want to create, and funds are secured through a P2P Bitcoin multisig escrow system.
