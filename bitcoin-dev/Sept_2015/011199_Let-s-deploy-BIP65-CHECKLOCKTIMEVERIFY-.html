A comment is made in agreement with Peter's points, stating that BIP65 should be deployed immediately and not wait for the 0.12 schedule. Gavin has agreed to support BIP65 in XT. Finally, there is a question posed about backporting BIP65 to 0.9, as some miners requested a backport during the deployment of BIP66, resulting in the creation of 0.9.5.
