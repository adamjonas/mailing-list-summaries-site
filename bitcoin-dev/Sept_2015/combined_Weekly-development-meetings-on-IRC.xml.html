Summary:

During a discussion on bitcoin-dev about scheduling a regular developer meeting, Matt Corallo suggested using a timezone that observes Daylight Saving Time (DST) in roughly the same way as everyone else. However, Gregory Maxwell pointed out that there is little global consistency in DST usage, and many places do DST on different dates. Btc Drak proposed selecting an actual timezone of an actual area so the meeting time would remain constant, such as 1700 CEST or 0900AM PDT. In response, Matt Corallo wondered if it was possible to select a timezone available in Google Calendar, since UTC wasn't directly supported. Wladimir J. van der Laan suggested scheduling the meeting every week from 15:00-16:00 UTC on Thursday.
