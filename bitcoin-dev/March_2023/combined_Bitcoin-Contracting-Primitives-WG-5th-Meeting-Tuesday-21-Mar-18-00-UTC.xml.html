Summary:

The 5th Bitcoin Contracting Primitives WG meeting is scheduled to take place on Tuesday, March 21st at 18:00. The meeting has been scheduled for the third Tuesday of every month and an open invitation is available for anyone who would like to propose an agenda topic in advance. Currently, the only discussion subject is pinned ANYPREVOUT/"Eltoo" but participants are encouraged to add more topics. Those interested in hosting the meeting are welcome to comment on the issue. It is a good practice to rotate hosts if possible.The communication venue for the meeting will be #bitcoin-contracting-primitives-wg on Libera Chat IRC. Logs of the previous session can be found on the group's GitHub page. Antoine, the proposer of the meeting, is available to answer any questions or feedback about the upcoming meeting. Anyone who wants to participate in the meeting can join the channel and share their thoughts. The meeting provides an opportunity to discuss important issues related to the Bitcoin community.
