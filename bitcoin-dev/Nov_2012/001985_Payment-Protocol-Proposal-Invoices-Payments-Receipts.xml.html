It has been suggested that supporting DNSSEC/DANE in the future would be a good idea. It is important to note that the x509chain field is "repeated" and may have zero entries, which means that other PKI systems should also be supported in the future. To achieve maximum compatibility or security, merchants may want to include both a x509chain and optional new fields.
