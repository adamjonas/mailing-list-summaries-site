Summary:

The poster is requesting a worked out scriptPubKey for doing multisig with just hashes of the participants. They believe it is more secure to a compromised ECDSA. The proposed scriptPubKey includes 2 OP_SWAP, 4 OP_HASH160, 3 OP_EQUALVERIFY, 2 OP_DUP, and 1 OP_CHECKMULTISIG. They are also requesting confirmation or a link to the worked out script for the scriptSig.
