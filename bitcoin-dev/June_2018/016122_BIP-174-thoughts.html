Meanwhile, the new Coldcard hardware wallet is based on PSBT (BIP 174) and is considered "PSBT Native", allowing it to add signatures to PSBT files from MicroSD or USB and finalize PSBT files for simple cases. The BIP174 spec is deemed reasonable and should only be changed in a forwards-compatible way moving forward. There are no strong feelings regarding specific comments made. Peter D.
