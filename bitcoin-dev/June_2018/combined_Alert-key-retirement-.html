The alert key for Bitcoin remains undisclosed despite the retirement of the alert system. The reason for this is that it has been suggested to wait for v0.13 end-of-life before revealing the alert keys, as per information provided on the Bitcoin website and the bitcoin-dev mailing list. The author of a recent message is seeking information about final alert messages requested by any projects that may have copied the Bitcoin alert pubkey.
