Summary:

The proposed Taproot soft fork has garnered attention from the community, with notable contributors such as David Harding, AJ Towns, Aaron van Wirdum, Jonathan Bier, Ben Carman, and Alejandro De La Torre working towards a consensus on the activation method. Various proposals for activating Taproot can be found on the Bitcoin wiki, Erisian, Bitcoin Magazine, BitMEX blog, Suredbits, and Taproot activation websites. To make progress towards finalizing the revised BIP 8 activation method, a meeting will be held on IRC on the ##taproot-activation channel on February 2nd at 19:00 UTC. Participants are encouraged to do pre-reading and relevant pull requests related to BIP 8 are available on GitHub. General questions or discussions regarding Taproot activation are welcome on the IRC channel before or after the meeting.
