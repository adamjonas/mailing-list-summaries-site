Mike Hearn, a software developer, added some additional logging to his node and ran it for a few days. There is a pull request open for his extra logging, which is quite trivial. He posted a log showing the transactions accepted by his node, and he suspects that the modified software is relaying transactions without checking their validity as they always win the race to be the first to announce to his node.
