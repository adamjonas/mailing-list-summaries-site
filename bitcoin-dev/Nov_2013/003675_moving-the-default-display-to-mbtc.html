Summary:

In a mailing list, Allen Piscitello suggested displaying all fees and trading rates in BTC or MBTC instead of using fractions of bitcoins, as it could be confusing to users. He also recommended that the transition should only happen once to avoid any damage. Another user suggested using SI prefixes, such as mega for MBTC (10^6), kilo for kBTC (10^3), milli for mBTC (10^-3), micro for uBTC (10^-6), and nano for nBTC (10^-9). The discussion aimed to find a solution that would simplify bitcoin transactions for users.
