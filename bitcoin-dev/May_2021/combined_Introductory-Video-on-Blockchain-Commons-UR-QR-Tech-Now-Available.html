Summary:

Blockchain Commons has been collaborating with the Airgapped Wallet Community to create interoperability specifications for enhancing Bitcoin wallet usage and expanding these to other cryptocurrencies such as Ethereum wallets and cryptography applications. The aim is to improve the independence, security, and resilience of cryptographic systems. An introductory video outlines the initiatives, including work on encoding methods, sharding methods, user interfaces, partitioned architectures, multisig patterns, and more.Blockchain Commons is developing new documentation for developers, starting with Uniform References (URs). URs are a typed method for encoding binary data in plain-text strings suitable for QR that are also well-formed URIs. Further documentation will cover using URs interactively and for animated PSBTs and shamir seed shares. Blockchain Commons encourages developers to get involved in using these specifications and associated technologies.The interested parties can join the likes of Fully Noded, AirGap Vault, BlueWallet, Cobo Wallet, and Sparrow Wallet. Relevant links to sites, information on security architectures and methods, education, and related libraries and applications are available for better understanding.
