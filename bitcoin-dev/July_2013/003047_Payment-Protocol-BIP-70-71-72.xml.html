Summary:

In an email conversation from August 1, 2013, E willbefull expressed that they believe PaymentRequest-only bitcoin URIs will become increasingly important. They argued that some types of payments (exotic transactions) may not make sense to have a single fallback address. However, Gavin Andresen pointed out that P2SH addresses already support all exotic transactions. Additionally, E willbefull suggested that a page with a bitcoin URI link may rely on a separate service provider to assemble the transaction. Gavin Andresen clarified that the payment transaction will always be created by the customer's wallet software.
