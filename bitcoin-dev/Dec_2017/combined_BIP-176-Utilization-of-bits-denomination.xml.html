Summary:

A possible solution to simplify the use of Bitcoin is to create a new unit of measurement called "coinbit", according to a proposal. This new unit would equal 1 million coinbits, making it easier for smaller denominations and calculations. The abbreviation "bit" could also be used as a shorthand for "coinbit". Although this proposal has not been widely adopted, it represents a potential solution for streamlining the use of Bitcoin in everyday transactions.
