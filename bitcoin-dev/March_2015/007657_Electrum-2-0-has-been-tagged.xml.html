Summary:

The best practice for shared wallet use case should be multi-device multi-sig, but in reality, people would prefer to have a pool of spending money that they can access easily from different devices such as their primary computer, games console, and while out and about. Thus, it may not always be practical to rely on multi-device wallets. There will always be a need for a convenient hot wallet that is synchronized between different devices.
