In an email to Paul Sztorc, Gregory Maxwell expresses concerns about the security risks of Drivechain, as well as its potential to damage both the main chain and other sidechains. He suggests that there are safer solutions for scaling Bitcoin and integrating it with other chains, and that a proper solution will likely present itself in due time. Maxwell also references a link to a previous email where he discussed his concerns. In response, Sztorc defends Drivechain, citing the support of Luke-Jr and Adam Back, and questions Maxwell's objections. The two also discuss the issue of roadmaps in Bitcoin development.
