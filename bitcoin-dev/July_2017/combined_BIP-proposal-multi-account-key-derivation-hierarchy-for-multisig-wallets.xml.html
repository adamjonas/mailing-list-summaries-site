Summary:

The author presents a new structure for multi-account multisig wallets that avoids the complexities of BIP45, which specifies a HD structure for multisig wallets. When used in a multi-account fashion, BIP45 becomes complicated because one must use either a new master for every multisig account or the same master for many multisig accounts, resulting in complex and undesirable key reuse.The proposed structure follows the derivation scheme of other proposals, such as BIP44 and BIP49, but adds a level to take into account multisig account separation. Users can create multiple multisig accounts and provide their account's public derivation to the cosigners. The proposed structure is m/purpose'/coin_type'/account'/cosigner_index/change/address_index.The creation of a multisig P2SH address will follow the same procedure as described in BIP45, with each cosigner selecting their branch from the other cosigners' trees. The author seeks feedback on whether this proposal would be acceptable as a BIP.
