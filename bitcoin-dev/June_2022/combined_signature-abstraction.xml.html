Summary:

The author's proposal is to create a protocol for signatures using bounded elliptic curve parameters in the script. This would enable efficient verification of a range of elliptic curves schnorr signatures, rather than being limited to a fixed curve. The author believes that this approach could lead to higher bitness, broad HSM compatibility, and decentralized and competitive security parameters for different environments.However, the author is unsure if anyone else has proposed this idea before and whether others would find it useful. Nonetheless, the author sees potential benefits in terms of improving security and efficiency in cryptography.
