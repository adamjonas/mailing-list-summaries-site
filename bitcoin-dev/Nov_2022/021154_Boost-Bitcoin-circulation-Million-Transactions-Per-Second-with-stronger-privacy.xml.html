Full Replace-By-Fee (RBF) is becoming the norm, and transactions without it can be rejected as part of the Sabu protocol. However, watchtowers can be used to fix this issue and prevent attacks in progress. Transactions can include a small watchtower fee for an always-on watchtower service. It is important for all parties to maintain Sabu state, which functions as another mempool.
