Summary:

When building a coin selection algorithm, there are three goals to consider: minimizing cost, maximizing privacy, and minimizing UTXO footprint. However, it is difficult to achieve all three goals simultaneously. A coin selection algorithm that minimizes cost may not necessarily minimize UTXO footprint, and one that maximizes privacy will do so at the expense of UTXO footprint and fees.
