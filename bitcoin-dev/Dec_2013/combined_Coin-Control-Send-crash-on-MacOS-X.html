Summary:

In December 2013, Warren Togami Jr. backported patches from master and Litecoin to create a Bitcoin 0.8 client with more features, which was successful on Linux and Win32. Unfortunately, recently the team discovered a rare crash that can happen on MacOS X involving SendCoinsEntry::clear(). The issue is reproducible on MacOS X 10.9 and 10.6.8.Warren has been unsuccessful at building master due to an unfamiliar build environment and is trying qt-4.8.5 next, but it will be difficult to reproduce the crash. Wladimir suggested removing the setFocus() calls that happen after Send is clicked, and moving them to a separate function "focusPayTo" to resolve the issue. However, this caused another crash within qt. The sender then tried replacing a loop that deletes widgets with ->deleteLater() to avoid any potential crashes caused by deleting a QObject while pending events are waiting to be delivered. Despite these attempts, the program still crashes due to something else within Qt.
