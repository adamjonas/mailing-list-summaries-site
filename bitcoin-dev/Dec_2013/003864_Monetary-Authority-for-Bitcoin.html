In an email exchange on December 10, 2013, Ryan Carboni proposed a solution for tracking exchanges in Bitcoin. He suggested that the exchanges could be hard-coded into the system or left up to the miner's choice. Gavin Andresen responded with a comparison to wanting a fast way to travel around the world without focusing on the difficult problem that needs to be solved for it to work.
