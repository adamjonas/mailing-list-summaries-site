Summary:

In 2013, Arto Bendiken discussed the similarity between Bitcoin's BIP process and Python's PEP process. The BIP process was directly based on Python's PEP process, with BIP 1 acknowledging that it heavily borrowed from PEP-0001. Additionally, other analogs to BIP were noted for enhancement proposal processes in XMPP and BitTorrent. These details were shared in an email thread and included attachments in both HTML and non-text formats.
