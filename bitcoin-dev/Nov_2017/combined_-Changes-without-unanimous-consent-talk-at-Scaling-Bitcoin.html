Summary:

During a Consensus stream talk at Scaling Bitcoin, ajtowns presented an analysis of split-related consensus changes. The slides and paper for the talk can be found on GitHub at https://github.com/ajtowns/sc-btc-2017/releases. In addition, ajtowns proposed code-less solutions for generic replay protection similar to BIP 115. Furthermore, he offered a way to improve price discovery for proposals that may cause splits.
