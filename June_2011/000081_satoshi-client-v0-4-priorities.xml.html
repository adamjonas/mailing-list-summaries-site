    Summary:

    The conversation between Doug Huff and Jeff Garzik on the Bitcoin-development mailing list revolves around wallet encryption, which is a "must-have" feature for version 0.4. Sipa had already rebased his showwallet tree which was missing the segfault fix but said fix merges cleanly. However, it is unclear whether it's ready for pulling, but it is in a functional state. Jeff Garzik emphasizes that wallet crypto and wallet import/export are essential features for v0.4 as they enhance wallet security. He believes that autotools and other stuff can wait if they hold up the release of wallet crypto. The email also includes an advertisement for Splunk, which makes sense of IT infrastructure data.
