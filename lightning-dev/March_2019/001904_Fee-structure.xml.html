Summary:

The discussion is about the fees for payments made through channels in Bitcoin's Lightning Network. The size of the payment does not affect the proportional fee paid, but rather how far along the channel the payment is being sent. The term "proportional" means that the fee increases based on the amount being forwarded. However, in this case, it also varies based on the state of the channel.
