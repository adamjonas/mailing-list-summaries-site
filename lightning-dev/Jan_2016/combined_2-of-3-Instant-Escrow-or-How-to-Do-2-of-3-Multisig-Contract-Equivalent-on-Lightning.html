Summary:

Lightning Network is planning to introduce 2-of-3 party escrow which will function the same as the use case for on-chain "2-of-3 multisig" escrow systems without any 3rd party being a custodian of funds. Lightning uses Bitcoin transactions and scripts, meaning it is possible to do nearly instant off-chain multisig "escrow" on decentralized Lightning. The design is useful for payments for goods and services that need a third-party mediator. By making multiple R-value hashes supported in HTLCs, something equivalent to "multisig" can be done where payments are conditional upon two out of three parties agreeing. This is to support conditional multiparty payments, e.g. 2-of-3 "escrow", which is one of the biggest use cases of bitcoin scripting today.
