Then, the attacker creates one small transaction with a slightly higher fee and watches the low feerate transaction and its children get evicted, causing the fees in the mempool to drop dramatically. To mitigate this problem and optimize the mempool to maximize miner profit, Bitcoin Core has implemented various measures which are detailed in a document uploaded on their developer wiki.
