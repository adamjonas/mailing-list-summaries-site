Summary:

The Lightning Network protocol development team has announced a meeting that is open to the public, scheduled for Monday, March 2nd on IRC. The agenda includes pull request reviews for five issues, including reply channel range simplification, BOLT11 additional and negative tests, stuck channels, Wumbo advisory for scaling confirmations, and Gitbook quick follow-up. Long-term updates include discussions on the current status of the trampoline routing proposal, protocol testing framework, rendez-vous, and improving gossip (`gossip_queries_ex`). The rendez-vous routing and trampoline routing proposals are currently being discussed in more detail. Links to the short agenda and discussion threads are provided in the original post.
