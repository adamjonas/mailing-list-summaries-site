Summary:

In a group discussion, CD proposed the idea of running LN on top of a plasma chain instead of the main chain. This would create a plasma layer-2 solution and LN layer-3 solution, which could be beneficial in the long-term future. The idea is to have a "full node" that contains at least one plasma chain and LN, alongside the main chain. This would eliminate the need for SPV or neutrino and allow for installing the "full node" on mobile phones.
