Summary:

The author of this post proposes a standard for the open source community to follow, which they call "The Raspberry Standard for Bitcoin." This standard dictates that Bitcoin and its associated applications should be light and optimized enough to run on a Raspberry Pi. The author goes into further detail about this standard in a blog post linked in the original message. They invite feedback and discussion from the community regarding their proposal.
