Summary:

In an email exchange, Thomas Voegtlin proposes an extension to BOLT-11 that allows invoices to contain two bundled payments with distinct preimages and amounts. The purpose of this proposal is to address the need for prepayment of mining fees in services like submarine swaps and JIT channels.Currently, services that require prepayment can ask for it when using dedicated client software, but competitors who do not require a dedicated wallet are vulnerable to DoS attacks and legal issues. By bundling the prepayment and main payment in the same invoice, service providers can ensure that both payments arrive before fulfilling the pre-payment HTLCs.This proposal aims to level the field of competition between Lightning service providers and allow non-custodian exchanges to take place without the need for a dedicated wallet. Thomas suggests implementing this change in BOLT-11 rather than using BOLT-12 or onion messages to keep things simple and non-interactive.
