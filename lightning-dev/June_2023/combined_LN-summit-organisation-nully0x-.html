Summary:

Antoine Riard has proposed to organize the LN Summit in Africa in June 2024. He believes that Africa is an ideal location for the summit due to its beautiful continent, rich cultural and historical past, fragmented financial systems, and a growing number of Lightning developers. However, there are potential operational challenges, such as visa travels and tropical weather, that need to be considered. To mitigate these challenges, Antoine suggests announcing the event well in advance and starting small by surveying countries with reasonable operational stability like Algeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Nigeria. The feedback from the survey will be shared privately with Lightning attendees at the end of the summer, and Antoine has already set up a dedicated communication endpoint for this purpose.
