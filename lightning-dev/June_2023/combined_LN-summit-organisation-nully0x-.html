The Lightning Dev community has proposed organizing the LN Summit in Africa in June 2024. The proposal highlights the reasons why Africa would be an ideal location for the summit, including its cultural and historical significance, the need for a compatible payment protocol to address financial system fragmentation, and the growing number of Lightning developers in the continent. The proposal also acknowledges the operational challenges that may arise, such as visa travels and tropical weather, and emphasizes the importance of transparency and fairness in the organizational process.To start the planning process, the proposal suggests surveying countries with operational stability, such as Algeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Nigeria. Additionally, there are existing Bitcoin and Lightning network communities in Africa, such as Qala Africa and BitDevs, which can be connected with to facilitate the planning process. The proposal encourages Lightning developers to get involved in the organization and highlights the opportunity for knowledge transfer between generations of contributors.
