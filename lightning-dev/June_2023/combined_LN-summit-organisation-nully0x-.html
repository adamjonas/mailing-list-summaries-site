In a recent email exchange on the Lightning-dev mailing list, Antoine Riard proposed organizing the LN Summit in Africa in June 2024. He highlighted several reasons for holding the summit in Africa, including the continent's rich cultural and historical past, fragmentation in its financial systems that could be solved with a compatible payment protocol, and the growing number of Lightning developers from Africa. Antoine emphasized the need for operational stability and transparency in organizing the event and suggested starting with a survey of countries such as Algeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Nigeria.
