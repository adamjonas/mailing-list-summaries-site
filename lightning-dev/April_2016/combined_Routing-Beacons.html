The inquiry made by Laurentmt is focused on the concept of routing based on beacons in the Lightning Network (LN). To ensure that this information is easily disseminated, the best-known route can be broadcasted. However, determining the "cheapest" route depends on the specific amount being transmitted, as rates are expressed as "base + per-satoshi-amount". Therefore, certain heuristics will need to be implemented to determine the best option.In addition to this inquiry, a LN newcomer is also seeking information on the use of routing based on beacons.
