Summary:

The Bitcoin-dev mailing list saw a discussion on the use of the collaborative path in Taproot, expressing concerns with its compatibility with update_tx. The conversation then moved towards potential optimizations unlocked by taproot/tapscript and a series of eltoo scripts passed around for private discussion. These scripts described an eltoo update transaction, settlement transaction, and HTLC claim/refund transactions that utilized muSig. The proposed UPDATE TX script was estimated to be about 282 weight units total. The conversation touched on various aspects of the proposed scripts, including combining multiple UPDATE TXs and SETTLEMENT TXs, adding fees, and calculating the private key using ECDH between peers to generate the seed. Overall, the scripts were considered more "thought bubble" than draft at the time of discussion.
