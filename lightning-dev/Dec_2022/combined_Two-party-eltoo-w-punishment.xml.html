Summary:

Eltoo is a layer 2 scaling solution for Bitcoin's Lightning Network that aims to make forwarding payments faster by limiting the publication of eltoo states through a cryptographic puzzle. The post on optimizing eltoo for the two-party scenario explains how Alice can publish an update transaction K, and then Bob can publish an update transaction K > N, where N represents the latest version of the channel. The symmetric transactions prevent cheating and ensure a strict bound on shared_delay encumbered in the on-chain publication of the channel. However, there are concerns over risks such as locked-up funds if the recipient goes offline and the possibility of Bob attempting to confirm his own unilateral close.The email exchange between Antoine Riard and Anthony Towns discusses various methods for claiming funds in a new state scenario when broadcasting UB.n and CA.n. One approach is to use an adaptor signature or CTV-like approach. Additionally, the "sig-in-the-script" trick may work if UB.n scriptpubkey contains the signature for CB.n, SB.n, RB.n. The challenge of paying watchtowers and ensuring fair and reliable compensation for their fees is also discussed, and a suggestion is made to give the watchtower a pre-signed transaction WA.n to set up a new lightning channel with initial capacity of "my funds."The post notes that extending eltoo to multiparty channels presents challenges such as penalizing being harder and coordinating multi-party state updates potentially more difficult. Other issues include how to add fees and pay watchtowers, lack of layered transactions, and extending to multiparty channels. The Lightning Network (LN) is exploring ways to optimize its eltoo mechanism for two-party channels. Each channel participant maintains five transactions that require different actions based on the state of the channel.In addition, the email discusses potential solutions for paying watchtowers in the Lightning Network while ensuring they are compensated fairly and reliably. One suggestion is to give the watchtower a pre-signed transaction WA.n, which includes an output for a new lightning channel between the user and the watchtower with an initial capacity of the user's funds. The post also notes that fast forwards protocol upgrade proposal based on update-turn in the LN-penalty paradigm could improve UX by reducing HTLC propagation latency.Overall, eltoo has the potential to simplify the implementation process for Lightning Network, but there are still challenges to overcome, particularly when it comes to extending it to multiparty channels.
