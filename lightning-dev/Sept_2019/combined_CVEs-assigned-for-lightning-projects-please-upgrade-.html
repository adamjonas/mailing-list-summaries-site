Summary:

The lightning projects used by various users have been found to have security issues that may lead to loss of funds. Details about these issues will be made public on September 27, 2019. To avoid potential loss of funds, users are advised to upgrade their systems before this date. One of the affected releases is CVE-2019-12998 c-lightning. Users should take immediate action to upgrade their system to avoid any risk. It is recommended that users take proactive measures such as upgrading their systems regularly, keeping their software up to date, and staying informed about any security concerns or updates to maintain the integrity of their financial transactions. Developers can also be contacted for assistance if users experience any trouble updating their implementation.
