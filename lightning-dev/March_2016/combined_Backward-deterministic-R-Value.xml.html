Summary:

The Lightning Network's use of R values in bipayment channels without HTLCs is discussed in several email conversations and posts on Lightning-dev. Nicolas Dorier and Rusty Russell discuss the idea of one way deterministic RValue generation, with shachain being a variant that avoids generating millions of hashes. Mats Jerratsch expresses concerns about security implications of disclosing R values for payments not in the current commitment, to which Nicolas Dorier clarifies that Shachain can be used to avoid generating too many hashes in advance. Additionally, the proposal suggests making the RValues backward deterministic. The email threads also highlight nomenclature clashes and limited understanding of the latest developments in Lightning technology.
