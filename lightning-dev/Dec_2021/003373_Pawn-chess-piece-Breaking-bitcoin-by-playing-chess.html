The email is inquiring about the email address needed to obtain approval or permission for opening and closing lightning network channels or performing OP_RETURN transactions with bitcoin. They provide links to two related things: Koala Studio's attempt to play chess on LN in 2019, which shutdown in August 2019, and Etleneum still having chess but working differently. Their primary goal with this project can be different and focused on testing Bitcoin transactions, while the secondary goal is to have fun and contribute to increasing demand for block space. If chess sounds boring, it can be replaced with any two-player game that works for such a setup and can be played with patience over a few hours/days. OP_RETURN is used by many projects and excluded from the UTXO set. The sender signs off with their name and public key.
