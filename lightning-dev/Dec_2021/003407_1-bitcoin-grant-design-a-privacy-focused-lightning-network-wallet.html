Summary:

A 1 bitcoin research grant has been announced for the design of a privacy-focused lightning network wallet. The design should incorporate existing privacy improvements such as Tor, Taproot and WabiSabi CoinJoin. The grant will offer 0.5 BTC upfront to the selected research team and another 0.5 BTC will be split between three individuals to form another research team. Those interested in applying can contact rafe@zksnacks.com. Further information is available at https://blog.wasabiwallet.io/1-btc-ln-privacy-grant/.
