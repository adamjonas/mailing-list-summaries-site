The Lightning Network's lightning protocol for network-protocol level involves Alice and Bob, who have a channel set up with fully signed commitment transactions. Alice proposes an unsigned transaction to update the channel to reflect a conditional payment from Bob once R is revealed. The process ensures that both parties have matching, updated, signed commitment transactions along with secrets necessary to void attempts to use older commitments. In case of cheating, either party can safely close the channel after any step.
