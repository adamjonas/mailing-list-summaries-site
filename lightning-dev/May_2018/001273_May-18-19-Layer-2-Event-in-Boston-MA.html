Summary:

The MIT Digital Currency Initiative and Fidelity Labs are hosting a two-day event on Layer 2 on May 18-19. The event will feature talks and panels from leading experts in the field, as well as an all-day hackathon at the MIT Media Lab. Day one of the event will be held at Fidelity Labs, with lively talks and panels followed by a reception. Day two will take place at the Media Lab's sixth floor and will feature a hackathon with breakfast, lunch, and a reception included. The event is free and open to all. Interested individuals can register through the event's website, http://www.bostonblockchaincommunity.com/.
