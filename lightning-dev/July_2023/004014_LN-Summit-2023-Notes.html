In late June, the annual specification meeting took place in New York City. The speakers are not identified, and any errors in the notes are solely the responsibility of the person who compiled them.Gratitude was expressed towards those who traveled long distances to attend the meeting, as well as Wolf for graciously hosting the event in NYC. Michael Levin also received thanks for assisting with the note-taking process.
