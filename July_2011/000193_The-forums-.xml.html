    A member of the bitcoin community expressed annoyance with the current forums and proposed that bitcoin.org replace the link to the forums with a more helpful and neutral forum for users, merchants, and developers. The discussion highlights the importance of having a helpful and moderated forum for the growing bitcoin community.
